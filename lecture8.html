Week 8 — Research Writing

In this session, i learned about research writing and how to properly present information in an academic way. The session explained the importance of having a clear structure, using reliable sources, and writing in a formal style to make research more professional and meaningful.


Key Learnings

From this lecture, I learned that research writing is not just collecting information but analyzing, organizing, and presenting it clearly. I understood the importance of citations, avoiding plagiarism, and following a proper format. This session showed me that good research writing builds credibility, improves communication, and helps to share knowledge effectively.
