Week 9 — Food Festival


On the food festival day our group ran the stall "WEIRDO’S SPOT" and sold Tteokbokki, Rolled Oreos, Choco Biscuit Pudding, and Blackcurrant Mojito.It was a so creative and practical activity where we were able to combine teamwork, culinary skills, and event management skills.


From this experience, I learned the importance of teamwork, planning, and time management. We understood how difficult it can be when choosing complex dishes, as it affected our preparation and sales. Those mistakes taught us valuable lessons about decision-making, dividing roles, and promoting our work. Even though we didn’t achieve the sales we expected, the experience gave us insight into handling real-life challenges, working under pressure, and communicating effectively within a team.

My Experience

For me, the Food Festival was one of the most enjoyable activities of the semester. Cooking together with my friends and managing our stall gave me a chance to build stronger friendships and develop practical skills. Even though we faced difficulties, I am glad we worked hard as a team, supported each other, and created something memorable. It was fun, exciting, and a great opportunity to learn beyond the classroom.
