Week 12 — Emotional Intelligence


We learned Emotional Intelligence in this session, which deals with understanding and managing our own emotions and the emotions of others. The session explained the difference between emotions and intelligence, how emotions affect our life daily, and why EQ is as important as IQ for personal success and professional growth.

Key Learnings

I understood from this lecture that emotions are not just feelings but they influence our behavior, relationships, health, and decision-making. EQ helps us to recognize emotions, use them positively, and regulate them in difficult situations. I also understood that people with high EQ can manage stress better, have good relationships, and succeed in teamwork and leadership. For me, this session opened up the fact that gaining Emotional Intelligence is extremely important in both academic and workplace settings, for the simple reason that technical knowledge is incomplete without emotional stability.
